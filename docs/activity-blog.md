## Definition of “Done”

A task is considered **Done** when:

- The planned work has been completed according to requirements
- Acceptance criteria are met
- Work is documented (README, wiki, or comments)
- The task has been reviewed by at least one team member
- The task has been moved to the **Done** column on the GitHub Projects board
